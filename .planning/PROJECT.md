# Awesome Legal Apps

## What This Is

A curated GitHub "awesome list" repository that collects high-quality legal tools, apps, AI models, skills, frameworks, and other resources for legal professionals and technologists. It follows the established awesome-list format — rigorously organized, thoroughly researched, and regularly maintained with community contributions in mind.

## Core Value

The definitive, most rigorously curated starting point for anyone exploring legal technology — where quality of curation beats quantity of links.

### Out of Scope

- Building any of the tools listed — curation only
- Real-time pricing or feature comparisons — links to sources instead
- Legal advice — resource directory, not a law firm

## Context

- The "awesome list" format on GitHub is a well-established convention (sindresorhus/awesome) with strict quality standards
- Legal tech is a rapidly growing space; existing lists like Lawvable exist but the landscape changes quickly
- Target audience: legal professionals, law students, legaltech developers, and researchers
- The repo should comply with the awesome-list standard to be eligible for inclusion in the main awesome index

## Constraints

- **Format**: Must follow sindresorhus/awesome conventions — README.md as the canonical list, with a license and contributing guide
- **Quality**: Each entry must be a working, maintained resource (no abandonware, no link rot)
- **Scope**: Legal domain only — tools that serve legal use cases, not general productivity tools
